Toronto Popular Landmarks

Toronto Island

Toronto Island is not like a normal island, when people think of islands they think of beaches or beautiful blue oceans. The islands are home to parkland, the Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport, several yacht clubs, Centreville Amusement Park, and Hanlan's Beach. The island community is considered to be the largest urban car-free community in North America, although some service vehicles are permitted. You can ride a bike around on the island. I know Its always cold in Toronto, but people still go to the island even when its snowing.
